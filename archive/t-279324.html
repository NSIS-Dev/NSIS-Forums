Currently, there's only .onSelChange. But you can one-up Afrow's script with LogicLib (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/LogicLib).!include LogicLib.nsh
!include Sections.nsh
#...
${If} ${SectionIsSelected} ${sec01}
${SelectSection} ${sec02}
${EndIf}
