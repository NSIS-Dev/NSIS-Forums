${IF} $R0 != 0
${ORIF} $R0 < ${VERSION}
StrCpy $outHandle 1
${ELSEIF} $R0 != ""
${ORIF} $R0 = ${VERSION}
StrCpy $outHandle 2
${ENDIF} You might also find that you need quotes around the $R0 operands.

$outHandle should be declared with 'Var'. ${outHandle} is declared with '!define' and probably won't let you StrCpy to it.
