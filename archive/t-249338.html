Currently, you can't stop an installation of an NSIS installer. I would use the nxs plug-in (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Nxs_plug-in), because this has cancel functionality. If you have many files in your install, I would suggest you write a small app like the one here (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/One_Installer_with_Different_Installation_Files_Each_Time). It makes a list of files as an NSIS header file, and is then included into the main installer. Add extra commands for the nxs plug-in in the script. This is just one solution, can't think of other solutions.
