SetOutPath "$INSTDIR\lib"

CreateDirectory "$SMPROGRAMS\example"

CreateShortCut "$SMPROGRAMS\example\Start SMail.lnk" \
"$5\bin\java.exe" \
'-jar -Duser.dir="$INSTDIR" "$INSTDIR\lib\example.jar" start' \
"$INSTDIR\example.ico" 0 SW_SHOWNORMAL




Try that.
