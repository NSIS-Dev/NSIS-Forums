Hi Steve,

After building your final installation file, you could then use a free program such as Resource Hacker (http://rpi.net.au/~ajohnson/resourcehacker/) to then modify the dialogs.

So you could then add the icons/bitmaps to the installer, and then place them on the windows.

One drawback is that you would have to turn off CRC checking. However, there are ways around that too.

Hope this helps!
