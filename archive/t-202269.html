This (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bitwise_operation) should get you started.StrCpy $0 0x06528000
IntOp $1 $0 & 0x0F000000
IntOp $1 $1 >> 24
IntOp $2 $0 & 0x00F00000
IntOp $2 $2 >> 20
IntOp $3 $0 & 0x000F0000
IntOp $3 $3 >> 16
IntOp $4 $0 & 0x0000F000
IntOp $4 $4 >> 12
DetailPrint $1.$2.$3.$4
