i suspect that some of the test computers display the icons at sizes larger than 32x32, so there's no way but creating larger versions of your icon. it usually helps to put the scaled up icon in one layer (of your image editor) and then to redraw it on a second layer. sometimes you just need to redraw the outlines. that said, it's always best to start your icon as vector graphics, though even downsized icons might need some extra work.

have you tried vector magic (http://vectormagic.com/) yet? it doesn't always produce good results, but you can usually proceed from there.
