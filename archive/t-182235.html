If your using the ModernUI in NSIS v2.0 the Finish page has at least two checkboxes you could modify to your liking and a web link that can be modified to your liking all without writing a custom page. As for the If statements; There was a Logic library supporting that functionality. It was written by dselkirk. You can find this plugin here (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/archive/viewpage.php?pageid=349).
