You have to buy a certificate and sign your installer to become a known and trusted publisher. You can also get a free certificate, self-generated or from a website, to be just a known publisher. MSDN has more information (http://msdn.microsoft.com/workshop/security/authcode/intro_authenticode.asp) on signing code.
