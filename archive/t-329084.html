NSIS doesn't support regular expression, but you may try these functions:
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/bb759983.aspx
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ff468910.aspx
Using FindFirst with wildcards, FindNext & FindClose.
Using plugin such as ZipDLL (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/ZipDLL_plug-in) or NsUnzip (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/NsUnzip_plugin).
Copyfile can also be used to copy folder: CopyFiles /silent $INSTDIR $TEMP.
