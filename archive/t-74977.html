Try changing StrLen to StrCpy. Everything should work then!

Of course, you could do it this way, too. Put this line at the start of your script.!Define APPNAME 'My New App' Then when you want to refer to it, use ${APPNAME}.MessageBox MB_YESNO 'Would you like to install ${APPNANE}?' IDYES NoAbortYou don't have to use APPNAME. Choose anything.
