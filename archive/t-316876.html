Ok. Found out how to do it:

Var WelcomeUser
!define MUI_WELCOMEPAGE_TEXT $WelcomeUser
!define MY_WELCOMEPAGE_TEXT "blahblah"

Function .onInit
StrCpy $WelcomeUser "${MY_WELCOMEPAGE_TEXT} blah."
FunctionEnd
