If you get the message box even though you have used the /S switch it means they put it in .onInit and didn't check for /S. This means they have not built their installer to be a silent installer. There is nothing you can do about it but ask them to make it possible, or edit the NSI source yourself and make yourself a custom installer.

You will really have to ask them about the port and the password becuase that has nothing to do with NSIS.

Try their IRC channel: http://jakarta.apache.org/tomcat/irc.html
