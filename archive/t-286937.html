On the other hand, if you want it to be conditional upon some file/registry on the target machine, put code in the onInit function to hide the section.

To hide a section, you can change the section text (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/Chapter4.html#4.9.13.3) to an empty string, or put a "-" in the front of the section string.
