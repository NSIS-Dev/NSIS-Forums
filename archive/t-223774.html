Note that the Linux ports are not "full" ports - essentially, they can only be used as crosscompilers. You still can't create installers for Linux with NSIS.

You can always try downloading the source and SCons and see if it'll build for you. I would think it's possible, though you may need to provide EXE stubs from a precompiled source.
