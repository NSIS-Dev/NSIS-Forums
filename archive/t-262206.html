You can't use that trick in NLF files. You'll have to change that string with the appropriate command, or LangString. On the NLF, before each line, the name of the internal LangString it fills is listed. Use that with the following:LangString ^SomeString ${LANG_FRENCH} "Installation ${MyCustomText} MyAppNAme"
