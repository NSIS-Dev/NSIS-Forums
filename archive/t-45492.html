ex.)
----------------------------------------------------------
Section "Install"
WriteINIStr "$WINDIR\win.ini" DirectDraw nohwblt "0"

....

Section Uninstall
WriteINIStr "$WINDIR\win.ini" DirectDraw nohwblt ""
---------------------------------------------------------

result

win.ini

[DirectDraw]
nohwblt=0
