Silent DllRegisterServer:
Exec "regsvr32.exe /s $SYSDIR\msstdfmt.dll"


Why not use NSIS "RegDLL"?
SetOutPath $SYSDIR
RegDLL "$SYSDIR\msstdfmt.dll"
