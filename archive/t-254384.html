kichik
29th August 2006, 14:13
Build a Unicode string and read it 2 bytes at a time.System::Call "*(&w1024 'hello')i.r0"
StrCpy $1 $0
System::Call "*$0(&i2.r2)"
${While} $2 != 0
DetailPrint $2
IntOp $0 $0 + 2
System::Call "*$0(&i2.r2)"
${EndWhile}
System::Free $1
