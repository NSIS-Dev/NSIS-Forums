Can't you just keep reading from File$R0, and increment $R0 until the read value == "" (or Error flag is set)

E.g.
StrCpy $R0 0
LoopRead:
IntOp $R0 $R0 + 1
ClearErrors
ReadINIStr $R1 "$EXEDIR\file.ini" "Filelist" "File$R0"
IfErrors DoneRead

# Do stuff here

Goto LoopRead
DoneRead:
