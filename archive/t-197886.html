On Windows 9x, you can see it in $WINDIR\wininit.ini. On NT it's in the registry under HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Session Manager\PendingFileRenameOperations. NSIS also prints out which files will be deleted on reboot.
