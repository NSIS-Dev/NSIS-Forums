The parser strips the quotes of parameters, so there's no difference between "ADVANCED" and ADVANCED. You must have had a typo somewhere. For example, this works:!define a a
!if ${a} == "a"
!error blah
!endif!if will be case insensitive, like !if and StrCmp in the next version. Thanks for the notice.
