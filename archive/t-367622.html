${__FILE__} will have "abc.nsi". If you want the base name:

!searchparse "${__FILE__}" "" "SCRIPT_BASENAME" ".nsi"
!warning "I did not enjoy ${SCRIPT_BASENAME} at all. Kind regards, the Compiler"
