You can't compress over 2GB of data into the installer itself. You can use one of the available solutions like CABSetup (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/CABSetup_plug-in) or an external archive with 7z.exe or some other archiving tool.

The first "m" of "mmaping" stands for memory and should be there.
