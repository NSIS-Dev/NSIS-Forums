I hope that you have some backgrounds on System plugin, here it goes:

1. Since you know the class get the window handle with FindWindow (http://msdn2.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms633499.aspx)

2. With the window handle, get its title text with GetWindowText (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/en-us/winui/winui/windowsuserinterface/windowing/windows/windowreference/windowfunctions/getwindowtext.asp).

Good luck.
