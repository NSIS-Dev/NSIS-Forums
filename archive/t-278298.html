Since 2.30, those have been moved to InstallOptions.nsh and are therefore documented under InstallOptions (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/InstallOptions/Readme.html), as mentioned in the MUI Readme (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/Modern%20UI/Readme.html). MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_* is the same as INSTALLOPTIONS_* and you can use either.
