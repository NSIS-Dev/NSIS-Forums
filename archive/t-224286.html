As of version 2.05, setting the selection flag for a section group should affect the contained sections. Maybe there's another problem in the script. Attach it so someone can take a look. This example script works for me:!include Sections.nsh

Name test
OutFile test.exe

Page components
Page instfiles

XPStyle on

ShowInstDetails show

SectionGroup /e a grp

Section a
SectionEnd

Section a
SectionEnd

Section a
SectionEnd

Section a
SectionEnd

SectionGroupEnd

Function .onInit
SectionGetFlags ${grp} $0
IntOp $1 ${SF_SELECTED} ~
IntOp $0 $0 & $1
SectionSetFlags ${grp} $0
FunctionEnd
