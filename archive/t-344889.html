I found out that it happen, if no replace found.
the fix, is to add some chat that will be always replaced (match to REGEX):
(i used _ char)

${REReplace} $0 "[^A-Z^a-z^0-9]" "123ABC456xcv_" "_" 1
MessageBox MB_OK 'fixed: $0'
