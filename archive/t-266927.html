Get the image HWND using ReadINIStr or MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_READ by reading the HWND value of the field. Then, simply use ShowWindow on the HWND you get to hide it.!include WinMessages.nsh
!insertmacro MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_READ $0 "customPage.ini" "Field 5" "HWND"
ShowWindow $0 ${SW_HIDE}
