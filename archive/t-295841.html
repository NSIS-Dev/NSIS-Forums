Hello,

The three defines:
MUI_COMPONENTSPAGE_SMALLDESC
MUI_COMPONENTSPAGE_NODESC
UMUI_COMPONENTSPAGE_BIGDESC
Must be declared before the insertion of the macro pages.
Otherwise they will be ignored
