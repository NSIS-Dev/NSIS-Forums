Try Process Explorer (http://www.microsoft.com/technet/sysinternals/utilities/ProcessExplorer.mspx). With Process Explorer look in the properties of rundll32.exe and in Image tab look the command line field.

e.g. Safely remove hardware:
"C:\WINNT\system32\RUNDLL32.EXE" shell32.dll,Control_RunDLL hotplug.dll
