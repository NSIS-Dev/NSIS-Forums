I didn't test, but this might help you:

CreateDirectory "$SMPROGRAMS\Example"
CreateShortCut "$SMPROGRAMS\Example\doclnk.lnk" "$INSTDIR\doc.doc" "" "$INSTDIR\myico.ico" 0
