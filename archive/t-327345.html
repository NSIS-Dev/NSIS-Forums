Getii (getii.com) is an installer wrapper written in NSIS. The Getii installer is used as a wrapper around your software installer and enables you to make money from your software by offering users, during the installation process, to use the Getii search tools.

Users in Control
There are no hidden installations, the Getii installer displays a clear message to users who can choose not to use the offered search tools. After the user makes his choice your software installer is downloaded and run. Users stay in full control of the installation process.

Get Paid
If you are distributing free software then get paid for it while keeping it free for users. No need for panhandling, begging for donations, for beer or for a cup of coffee.

And Faster Downloads
Getii improves software distribution performance. The Getii wrapper is a very small installer stub that dynamically downloads your software installer. Downloads are made from a fast CDN network which results in a faster installation process.
