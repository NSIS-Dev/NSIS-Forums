I am using the system plugin to do that:System::Call 'kernel32::MoveFile(t "<Full_Path_of_Folder_to_rename>", t "<Full_Path_of_Folder_to_rename>.old")i r0'Note that $0 will be 0 if the call fails. You may want to look at MoveFileEx for more advanced features.
