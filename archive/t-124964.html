14th February 2003, 22:41
Replace:

SectionSetFlags ${uavs} ${readonly}

with:

SectionGetFlags ${uavs} $R9
IntOp $R9 $R9 & 0xFFFFFFEF
SectionSetFlags ${uavs} $R9

That will make it selectable by the user.
