Hello,

It is a known issue.

This is a workaround:
Simply, add this section before all others in your code:
Section ""
SetDetailsPrint none

Push $0
Push $8

FindWindow $0 "#32770" "" $HWNDPARENT
GetDlgItem $8 $0 1004
System::Call "user32::InvalidateRect(i,i,i)i (r8, 0, 1).r4"

Pop $8
Pop $0

SetDetailsPrint both
SectionEnd
