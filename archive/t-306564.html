Use of INI files with InstallOptions is deprecated. It's recommended you use nsDialogs instead.

If you look at the nsDialogs readme file (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/nsDialogs/Readme.html) (also located in your NSIS installation directory under \docs\nsdialgs), you will find a very good step by step tutorial on how to make it work. There is also a whole page of examples on this wiki page: http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Category:NsDialogs_Examples.

If you insist on using the older InstallOptions, then you may want to have a look at this page: http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Adding_custom_installer_pages.
