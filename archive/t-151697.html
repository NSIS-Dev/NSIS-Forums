I.e.:


LangString ORGANIZATION_NAME ${LANG_ENGLISH} "Organization Name"
LangString ORGANIZATION_NAME ${LANG_PORTUGUESEBR} "Nome da Organização"

Section

...

WriteINIStr "filename.ini" "Field 1" "Text" "$(ORGANIZATION_NAME)"

...

SectionEnd
