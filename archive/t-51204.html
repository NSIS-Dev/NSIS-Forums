try it like this:


Function FindWinAndWait
StrCpy $0 window_class_or_title
FindWindow Goto:lbl_found $1
;FindWindowByTitle Goto:lbl_found $1

Goto lbl_end

lbl_found:
Sleep 500
Call FindAndWait

lbl_end:
FunctionEnd

Exec whatever
Sleep 1000
Call FindWinAndWait
