IfFileExists "$EXEDIR\TagCD2.exe" FT TG
TG:
MessageBox MB_OK|MB_ICONINFORMATION "Insert CD 2"

FT:

Change it like this (the prompt is always shown once, and shown again until the CD is inserted):

IfFileExists "$EXEDIR\TagCD2.exe" FT TG
