Okay, after playing arround a little, I found a working solution:

SetOutPath "$INSTDIR\..\"
Rename "OP-Buch" "OP-Buch-Backup-${PRODUCT_VERSION}"
Rename "OP-Buch-${PRODUCT_VERSION}" "OP-Buch"
