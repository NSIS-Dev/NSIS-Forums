StrCpy $1 $INSTDIR 2
System::Call 'kernel32::GetDiskFreeSpaceEx(t, *l, *l, *l)i(r1,.r2,.,.) .r3'

If $3 does not equal 0, $2 contains the number of free bytes. See msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/aa364937(VS.85).aspx for details.
