Use InstType function

InstType "Full"
InstType "Tipical"

When you declare sections use SectionIn function with indexes of sections:

Section "Main"
SectionIn 1 2
...
SectionEnd

Section "Second"
SectionIn 1
...
SectionEnd

Look at the documentation about these functions.
