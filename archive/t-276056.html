kichik

Use WinVer.nsh to check the version and call Abort in .onInit if it doesn't match.!include WinVer.nsh
Function .onInit
${Unless} ${IsWinXP}
MessageBox MB_OK "XP required"
Abort
${EndIf}
FunctionEnd
