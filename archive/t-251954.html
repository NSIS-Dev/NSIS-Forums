Not sure if there is a better way, but this should do as long as every Field has a State:
StrCpy $0 1
ClearErrors
Read:
ReadINIStr $1 "<ini_path>\<ini_file>" "Field $0" "State"
IfErrors Stop
IntOp $0 $0 + 1
Goto Read

Stop:
IntOp $0 $0 - 1
DetailPrint "Number of fields: $0"
