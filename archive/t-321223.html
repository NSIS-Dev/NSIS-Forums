Have you tried to use IntOp on it?

IntOp $1 $0 +1
IntOp $1 $1 -1
${If} $1 == $0
; is an integer
${EndIf}

Something like the above should work. Strangely, the manual doesn't state what happens if you intop a non-integer... But just play around and you'll figure it out.
