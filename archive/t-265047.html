Seems like you don't have mingw installed. You must have mingw installed to cross compile the plug-ins. You can, however, skip the cross compilation, use a prebuilt package and compile just makensis as instructed in the documentation (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/AppendixG.html#G.3).scons SKIPSTUBS=all SKIPPLUGINS=all SKIPUTILS=all SKIPMISC=all NSIS_CONFIG_CONST_DATA_PATH=no
