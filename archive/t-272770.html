nsExec::Exec ignores the output, nsExec::ExecToLog shows the output in your installer log and nsExec::ExecToStack pushes the output to the stack.

As the documentation says:All functions are the same except ExecToLog will print the output to the log window and ExecToStack will push up to ${NSIS_MAX_STRLEN} characters of output onto the stack after the return value.
