after a quick test, it seems to work however... (i wonder how ^^)
OutFile "test.exe"
Var myvar
Section
StrCpy $myvar "foo"
MessageBox MB_OK "$myvarbar"
SectionEnd

if you feel troubled by this, you might want to use $0..$9 or $R0..$R9 instead of a named variable
