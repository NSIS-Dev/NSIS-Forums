You may (or may not) have realised that in the readme it says:
MUI_FINISHPAGE_RUN exe_file

xls is not an exe, and thus you should use:
MUI_FINISHPAGE_SHOWREADME file/url
MUI_FINISHPAGE_SHOWREADME_TEXT text
