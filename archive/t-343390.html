http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/Chapter4.html#4.9.5.12

loop:
FindFirst $0 $1 $INSTDIR\*.php
StrCmp $1 "" done
StrCpy $2 $1 -4
Rename "$INSTDIR\$1" "$INSTDIR\$2"
FindClose $0
Goto loop
done:
