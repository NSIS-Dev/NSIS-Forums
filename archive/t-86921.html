today was my first experience with NSIS and i must say, it rocks!!! i love the way that you've combined the conciseness and simplicity of packed instructions with the intricate level of detail and power of a pseudo-assembly language. great combination.

i would like to see the assembly portion extended a bit, but it was a good exercise for the mind to have to write some assembly without certain instructions (e.g., mov, cmp, and dereferencing by index). although, when i found out that strcpy had an offset param and various other workarounds, it was all systems go.

once again,
mad props to guys at NULLSOFT!!!
