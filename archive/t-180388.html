SubSection "Uninstall"

Section "Full" g1o1

SubSectionEnd

SubSection "Uninstall"

Joost Verburg
18th May 2004, 14:21
The uninstaller sections should start with the .un prefix.

The section called "Uninstall" will always be executed and is hidden (it's the same as a hidden section with the un. prefix).
