WISE did not use full-window skinning, so the ExperienceUI wouldn't work here. As kichik said your best bet is probably the InstallSpider UI.

Almost forgot: Defining XPUI_FULLBODY_SKIN will (contrary to the name of the define) disable the ExperienceUI's internal skinning support, allowing for an un-skinned installer or the use of an external plugin such as WAnsis.
