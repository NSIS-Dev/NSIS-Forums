This works ok, in my XP box:

Name "png_size"
OutFile "png_size.exe"
ShowInstDetails show

Function FileSizeNew

Exch $0
Push $1
FileOpen $1 $0 "r"
FileSeek $1 0 END $0
FileClose $1
Pop $1
Exch $0

FunctionEnd

Section -
Push "C:\joel\sshot-1.png"
call FileSizeNew
Pop $0
DetailPrint "$0 bytes"
SectionEnd
