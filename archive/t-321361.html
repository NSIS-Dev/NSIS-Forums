This is most probably caused by your virusscanner. When windows tries to access the .exe file to determine its icon, the virusscanner kicks in and locks the file until it has scanned it for viruses. Either be patient and wait till windows can access the icon (if ever), or set your virusscanner to not scan files larger than, say, 100MB.
