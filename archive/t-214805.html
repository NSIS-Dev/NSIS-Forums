Use:
SectionGetFlags ${SECID} $R0
IntOp $R0 $R0 & ${SF_SELECTED}
StrCmp $R0 ${SF_SELECTED} +2
Abort

Place this in your page's PRE function.
Make sure you !include WinMessages.nsh

${SECID} is defined by:
Section "a section" SECID
SectionEnd
