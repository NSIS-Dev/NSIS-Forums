The BIF_USENEWUI (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/default.asp?url=/library/en-us/shellcc/platform/shell/reference/structures/browseinfo.asp) has its limits.
That means the button appears only version 5 or higher of the Shell32.dll or Shlwapi.dll.
What NT version is running from?
