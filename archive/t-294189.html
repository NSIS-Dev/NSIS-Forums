zeeh3 asked me to add AnimateWindow effects to NewAdvSplash (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/NewAdvSplash_plug-in), but because of "window region" compatibility problems I wrote new Animate (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Animate_plug-in) plug-in instead.
