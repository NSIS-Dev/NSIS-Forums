Anders
18th August 2011, 23:25
This seems to work for me:

Function LeaveFunc
System::Call 'USER32::LoadCursor(i,i ${OCR_NO})i.s'
System::Call 'USER32::SetCursor(is)'

Sleep 5555

FunctionEnd
