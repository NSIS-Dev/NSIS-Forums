You need to use the LB_ADDSTRING (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/en-us/shellcc/platform/commctls/listboxes/listboxreference/listboxmessages/lb_addstring.asp) (ListBox) or CB_ADDSTRING (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/default.asp?url=/library/en-us/shellcc/platform/commctls/comboboxes/comboboxreference/comboboxmessages/cb_addstring.asp) (ComboBox) with SendMessage. There are also DELETESTRING messages.
