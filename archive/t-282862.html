A new version of the Registration plugin has been released. You can get the URL on the NSIS wiki in the Plugins section.

It takes advantage of the nsDialogs plugin. If you're curious about this plugin but don't know where to start it makes a good example of how to produce interactive screens in your installer.

It's also not a bad place to start if you need to validate serial numbers and registration keys in your software.
