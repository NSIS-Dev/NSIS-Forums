You can also use pre-compiler directives to use defines to enable functions that are needed.

## Uncomment to Enable "MyFunction"
; !define Enable_MyFunction

!ifdef Enable_MyFunction
Function MyFunction
## Do Stuff
FunctionEnd
!endif
