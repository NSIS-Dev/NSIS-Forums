If you just want to create a new text file e.g.
FileOpen $0 '$EXEDIR\file_name.txt' w
FileWrite $0 'a_line_of_text$\r$\n'
FileWrite $0 'more_line_of_text$\r$\n'
FileClose $0
ExecShell open '$EXEDIR\file_name.txt'
