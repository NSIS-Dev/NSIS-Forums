In .onInit function define your $ANOTHER_DIR variable.
For standard or preferencedirectory:
StrCpy $ANOTHER_DIR $INSTDIR
For c:\Programm\My Prog:
StrCpy $ANOTHER_DIR "c:\Programm\My Prog"
