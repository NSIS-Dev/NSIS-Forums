No, "$$%foo%" does not work.
This is how I solved it:!searchreplace MyOptionalEnv "$%MyOptionalEnv%" "%" "#"
!if "${MyOptionalEnv}" == "$#MyOptionalEnv#"
!echo "MyOptionalEnv not found"
!else
!echo "MyOptionalEnv found: ${MyOptionalEnv}"
!endifPlease note that this check runs at compile time, but since you mentioned $%MyOptionalEnv%, I assumed that is what you want.
