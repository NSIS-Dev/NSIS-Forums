Use SystemParametersInfo (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/default.asp?url=/library/en-us/sysinfo/base/systemparametersinfo.asp) from user32.dll to set the wallpaper:!define SPI_SETDESKWALLPAPER 20
!define SPIF_SENDCHANGE 2
WriteRegStr HKCU "Control Panel\Desktop" "WallpaperStyle" "2"
System::Call 'user32::SystemParametersInfo(i ${SPI_SETDESKWALLPAPER},i 0,t "$PROGRAMFILES\Waza\waza-1024x768.bmp",i ${SPIF_SENDCHANGE})i.r0'
