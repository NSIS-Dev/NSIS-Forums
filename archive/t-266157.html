ok i got a solution, finally. (thank god, and all how helped me further :-))
i copied the text after the FILE commands and now it works.

${Switch} $LANGUAGE
${Case} ${LANG_ENGLISH}
Delete $INSTDIR\speech.dcp
Rename "$INSTDIR\speech_eng.dcp" "$INSTDIR\speech.dcp"
${Break}
${EndSwitch}
