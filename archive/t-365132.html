i'm well aware of the halibut generator, but still .chm is a rather closed format. a personal favourite of mine would be markdown (http://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/), though even more than for a manual it's an ideal format for 3rd party addon developers. writing a proper documentation is often the part where developers get lazy and having to write two versions (distributed and wiki) doesn't help it. markdown is very readable in plain-text and there are many ways to render them in the browser to html, or use it for cms platforms and even wikis.
