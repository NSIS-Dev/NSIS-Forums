Mark -- Great idea. Now I copy the INI to a temp file, read it, and then delete the temp file. Works great.

GetTempFileName $R0
CopyFiles "my.ini" $R0
ReadINIStr $0 $R0 "My Section" "MyName"
Delete $R0
