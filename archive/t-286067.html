Afrow UK
9th April 2008, 16:10
GetDlgItem $R0 $HWNDPARENT 1
EnableWindow $R0 0
GetDlgItem $R0 $HWNDPARENT 2
EnableWindow $R0 0
GetDlgItem $R0 $HWNDPARENT 3
EnableWindow $R0 0

1 is the Next button, 2 and 3 are the Cancel and Back buttons.
