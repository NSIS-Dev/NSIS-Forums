You've already got what you need to distinguish them by using the handle, though?


Function settext.onChange
Pop $hwnd
${NSD_GetText} $hwnd $R8
${Select} $hwnd
${Case} $smtphwnd
MessageBox MB_OK "From smtphwnd: $R8"
${Case} $fromhwnd
MessageBox MB_OK "From fromhwnd: $R8"
${EndSelect}
FunctionEnd
