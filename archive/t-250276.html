EBanner displays image on existing window only. I guess Stu wrote about Start->Programs->NSIS->Contrib->Banner.
But another plug-in also available from archive - banner with progress bar and cancel button NXS (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Nxs_plug-in). To use plug-in just put it's dll to NSIS\plugins folder. Use ReserveFile if you need this at the very beginning of installation.
