kichik
1st April 2006, 11:44
Use CopyFiles to copy files on the user's machine and not from the developer's machine. Use NSISdl or InetLoad to download files off the internet. To unzip, use one of the available plug-ins (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Special:Search?search=unzip). There are also plug-ins for tarballs and for cabs.

Start->Programs->NSIS->Contrib-NSISdl or InetLoad (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/InetLoad_plug-in), both waits for download to be finished. Search the Forums link above will give you a lot of samples for files unzipping, but NSIS internal compressors are good enougth to not use zip.
