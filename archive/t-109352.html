Use InstallOptions. InstallOptions gives you the ability to create custom pages during installation. It can also get input for you such as another directory.

If you want to show one custom page right after the other use MultIni4 (http://www.clantpa.co.uk/nsis/wiki/index.php/MultIni4) or the new macros in the modern UI (avaiable in the latest CVS version).
