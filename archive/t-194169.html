You could use something like this:


!macro DetailPrint TEXT
SetDetailsPrint both
DetailPrint "${TEXT}"
SetDetailsPrint none
!macroend
!define DetailPrint "!insertmacro DetailPrint"

Section -blah
${DetailPrint} "Hello!"
SectionEnd
