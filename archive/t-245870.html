It may be a bit more complicated than you think ...
I am under the impression that you need to:
1) call LogonUserAPI (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/default.asp?url=/library/en-us/secauthn/security/logonuser.asp) and get a token
2) [maybe you don't need this step] call DuplicateTokenExAPI (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/default.asp?url=/library/en-us/secauthz/security/duplicatetoken.asp) in order to duplicate the token to a new one with the required access rights
3) call LoadUserProfile (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/default.asp?url=/library/en-us/policy/policy/loaduserprofile.asp) using the token from step 2
4) call UnloadUserProfile (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/en-us/policy/policy/unloaduserprofile.asp) after you're done.

There is a microsoft article (http://support.microsoft.com/default.aspx?scid=kb;en-us;196070) that discusses something similar to the above

Hope this helps
