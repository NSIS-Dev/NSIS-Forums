It's not so hard, and it actually IS in the documentation. But it is very hard to find ;). You can find it in Chapter 5 (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/Chapter5.html#5.2) under the header: !insertmacro.

This is an example line:
!insertmacro MUI_LANGUAGE "English"
Here, MUI_LANGUAGE is the name of the macro, and "English" is a parameter.
