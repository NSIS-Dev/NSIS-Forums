You can use SetPriorityClass (http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/en-us/dllproc/base/setpriorityclass.asp?frame=true):!define NORMAL_PRIORITY_CLASS 0x00000020
!define IDLE_PRIORITY_CLASS 0x00000040
!define HIGH_PRIORITY_CLASS 0x00000080
!define REALTIME_PRIORITY_CLASS 0x00000100

System::Call "kernel32::GetCurrentProcess() i .s"
System::Call "kernel32::SetPriorityClass(i s, i ${HIGH_PRIORITY_CLASS})"
