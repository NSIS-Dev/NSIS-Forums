You can also define date with your own format...

!define /date MYDATE "%b %dnd, %Y"
${MYDATE} = "April 30nd, 2008"

See strftime at MSDN for all symbols.
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/fe06s4ak(VS.71).aspx
