Exec, ExecWait, ExecShell: These are NSIS commands and are documented in the help (either with NSIS you downloaded or you can view the most upto-date CVS specific latest copy here (http://nsis.sf.net/Docs)).
nsExec::Exec, nsExec::ExecToLog, nsExec::ExecToStack: These are plugin functions of the nsExec plugin which is documented in your contrib/nsExec/nsExec.txt file.
