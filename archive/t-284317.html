If you don't want to skip the page (maybe a better idea...)

!include LogicLib.nsh
!include Sections.nsh

Section "My Section" MySection1
SectionEnd

...
${Unless} ${FileExists} $INSTDIR\xyz.txt
SectionSetFlags ${MySection1} ${SF_RO}
...
${EndUnless}
