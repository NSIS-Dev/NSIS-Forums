/s makes the installer silent already, but for /auto
you can do this in your .onInit function...
Function .onInit
Push $R0

Call GetParamaters
Pop $R0

Push $R0
Push "/auto"
Call StrStr
Pop $R0

StrCmp $R0 "" +2
SetSilent silent

Pop $R0
FunctionEnd

Both functions are in the NSIS documents section C.4
