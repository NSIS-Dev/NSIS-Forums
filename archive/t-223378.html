System plug-in calls work without 'A' in the last NSIS versions

Section
System::Call 'kernel32::GetModuleFileName(i 0, t .r0, i 1024) i r2'
System::Call 'kernel32::GetLongPathName(t r0, t .r1, i 1024)i .r2'
MessageBox MB_OK $1
SectionEnd
