Unfortunately, it doesn't appear that InstallOptions allows you to use the Notify flag with text boxes.

However, you should be able to accomplish this by using nsDialogs (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/nsDialogs/Readme.html) instead of InstallOptions. (This also comes stock with NSIS)

(Or, there's also the InstallOptionsEX (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/InstallOption****plug-in) plugin)
