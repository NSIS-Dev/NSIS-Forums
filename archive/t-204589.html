Bit like this:


Push $R0

GetDlgItem $R0 $HWNDPARENT 1
SendMessage $R0 ${WM_SETTEXT} 0 "STR:Install"

Pop $R0

...

...

!insertmacro MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_WRITE "ioUpgrade.ini" "Settings" "NextButtonText" "$(nextbut_txt)"
