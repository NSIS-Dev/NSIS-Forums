Nevermind, figured out with the help of ResHack..


nsDialogs::CreateControl /NOUNLOAD EDIT "${WS_VISIBLE}|${WS_CHILD}|${WS_TABSTOP}|${WS_VSCROLL}|${ES_MULTILINE}|${ES_LEFT}"
