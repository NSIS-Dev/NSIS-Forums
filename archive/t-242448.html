You'll need to extract the files to a temporary folder, loop through all files and folders recursively (use RecFind (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/RecFind:_Recursive_FindFirst%2C_FindNext%2C_FindClose) writing to $UninstLog: FileWrite $UninstLog "...$\r$\n"
and then Rename each file to its new location.
