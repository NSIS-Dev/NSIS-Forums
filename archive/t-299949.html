IMHO, if your click-able line is longer than 80 characters, then something is wrong with your choice of text for the link.

However, if you still believe that you must have the finish page link longer than NSIS MUI2 allows, then the only other advice I can give you is to use a custom finish page with the Linker plug-in (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Linker_plug-in).
