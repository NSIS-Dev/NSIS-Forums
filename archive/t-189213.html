The value you pass to InstallDir using:InstallDir "C:\something\blah"is processed when the installer loads, before .onInit so you can use $INSTDIR in .onInit. You should therefore copy the new value directly to $INSTDIR using:StrCpy $INSTDIR $InstallDirand not copy it to $R8.

You can also use InstallDirRegKey and then tweak $INSTDIR in .onInit instead of reading the registry yourself.
