Why would you like to pre-check the checkbox? That would make the checkbox pretty useless. The idea of the checkbox is to force the user to actively agree on something instead of just hitting enter.

However, if that's what you want, use FindWindow, GetDlgItem and SendMessage on the show callback function of the license page. This thread (http://forums.winamp.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=185213&highlight=checkbox+SendMessage) explains how.
