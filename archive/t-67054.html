Since CopyFiles uses SHFileOperation it's possible to do things like

Name "Test"
Outfile "Test.exe"

Section "Test"
CopyFiles "D:\TEST\*" "C:\"
SectionEnd

All files, subdirectories and files in subdirectories under D:\TEST\ will be copied to C:\
