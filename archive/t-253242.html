With plugins, anything in NSIS is possible.

Something like this:
FindFirst $0 $1 "$INSTDIR\plugins\*.dll"
loop:
StrCmp $1 "" done
RegDLL "$INSTDIR\plugins\$1"
;UnRegDLL "$INSTDIR\plugins\$1" ; UnRegDLL for uninstaller
FindNext $0 $1
Goto loop
done:
FindClose $0
