Your System call is overwriting $0 with the GetWindowLong result.

Try changingSystem::Call "user32::GetWindowLong(i $0, i -21) i.r0"
IntCmp r0 0xFF00FF00 0 loop looptoSystem::Call "user32::GetWindowLong(i r0, i -21) i.r1"
IntCmp $1 0xFF00FF00 0 loop loop
