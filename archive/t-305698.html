Yep, that's what I'm doing.

for the curious;

!macro myDetailPrint str
SetDetailsPrint both
DetailPrint "${str}"
SetDetailsPrint lastused
!macroend
!define myDetailPrint `!insertmacro myDetailPrint`

Section
SetDetailsPrint none
...
${myDetailPrint} "Hello world"
...
SetDetailsPrint both
SectionEnd
