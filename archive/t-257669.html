kichik
16th October 2006, 17:48
It's all in the documentation (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/AppendixE.html#E.1.21).!include FileFuncs.nsh
!insertmacro RefreshShellIcons
!insertmacro un.RefreshShellIcons

Section something
${RefreshShellIcons}
SectionEnd

Section uninstall
${un.RefreshShellIcons}
SectionEnd
