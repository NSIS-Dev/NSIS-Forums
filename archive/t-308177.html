You can enlarge fields by editing NSIS\contrib\UltraModernUI\UMUI.nsh

Into macro !macro UMUI_SERIALNUMBERPAGE_CREATE ID STR FLAGS DEFAULT TEXT WIDTH
replace line (6910 in the last build)
IntOp $1 $2 * 6
by
IntOp $1 $2 * 7
