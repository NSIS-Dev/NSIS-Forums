You can call SetWindowPos using the System plug-in and pass HWND_TOPMOST to it.!define HWND_TOPMOST -1
!define SWP_NOSIZE 1
!define SWP_NOMOVE 2
System::Call "user32::SetWindowPos(i $HWNDPARENT, i ${HWND_TOPMOST}, \
i0, i0, i0, i0, i ${SWP_NOSIZE}|${SWP_NOMOVE})"
