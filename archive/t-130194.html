I completely re-wrote the script, and got a far better script than from before.

Usage:
Push "My name is Afrow UK, and I love NSIS"

Function GetFirstStrPart
Exch $R0
Push $R1
Push $R2
Push $R3
StrLen $R3 $R0
IntOp $R3 $R3 + 1
loop:
IntOp $R3 $R3 - 1
StrCpy $R2 $R0 1 -$R3
DetailPrint $R2
StrCmp $R2 "" exit
