You shouldn't modify the function. Simply call it, and act as you wish according to its result. For example:!include LogicLib.nsh
Function .onInit
Call IsUserAdmin
Pop $0
${If} $0 != "true"
MessageBox MB_OK|MB_ICONSTOP "not admin"
Quit
${EndIf}
FunctionEnd
