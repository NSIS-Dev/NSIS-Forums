kichik

The uninstaller's $INSTDIR is the directory it starts in. If you put it in a sub-directory then the $INSTDIR reported in the uninstaller will be $INSTDIR\subdir. To get $INSTDIR use GetParent (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/AppendixB.html#B.1)
