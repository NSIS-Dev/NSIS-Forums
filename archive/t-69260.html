petersa

No way other than modifying the source. There's space in the setup header for two 358-byte bitmaps, so they have to be 16-colour.

You could probably make your high colour bitmaps though, note the size of them, and then plough through the source looking for the number 358, and replacing that with the size of your bitmap.
