To add an edit box, you can use InstallOptions or nsDialogs. Both have examples in the Examples folder.

To replace a line in a file, you can use one of the functions Yathosho posted, or LineFind (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Docs/AppendixE.html#E.2.2).
