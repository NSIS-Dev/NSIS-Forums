Shortcuts are .lnk files saved in folder (e.g. Desktop).
Simply search for all *. lnk files in desired directory and do your action with shortcut you want:

FindFirst $0 $1 $DESKTOP\*.lnk
loop:
StrCmp $1 "" done
DetailPrint $1
FindNext $0 $1
Goto loop
done:
FindClose $0
