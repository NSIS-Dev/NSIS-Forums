You need to use a GUID in the following syntax:

::{GUID}

The GUID of My Computer is 20D04FE0-3AEA-1069-A2D8-08002B30309D, so this should do the trick:

Root=::{20D04FE0-3AEA-1069-A2D8-08002B30309D}
