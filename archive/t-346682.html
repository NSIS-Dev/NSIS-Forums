Actually the full signature now is:extern "C" void __declspec(dllexport) name(HWND hWndParent, int string_size, TCHAR* variables, stack_t** stacktop, extra_parameters* extra)(backwards compatible). As for your question, the parameters you pass in the script are not passed as parameters to the plug-in function. Instead they are passed via the NSIS stack. You use popstring to grab them.

Have you had a look at the System plug-in? You can call normal DLLs with that; would save you converting your one to an NSIS plug-in.
