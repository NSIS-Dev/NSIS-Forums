I don't know ready solutions, this may be a combination of improved NewAdvSplash (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/wiki/AdvSplash_with_gif_and_jpeg) (in the /BANNER mode) and Marquee (http://nsis.sourceforge.net/wiki/Marquee_text_scroll) plug-ins, but some new code required.
Minimum requirements for your task are font type, size, chartset and color (italic, bold, etc.). And text may still looks ugly near splash image (if your image is really good :) )
