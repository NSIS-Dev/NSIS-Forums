You can easily convert it to one parameter while keeping NSISdl backward compatible and with semi-clean code.

!define NSISdl_trans /TRANSLATE '"$(T1)" "$(T2)" "$(T3)" "$(T4)" "$(T5)" "$(T6)" "$(T7)" " $(8)"'
NSISdl::download ${NSISdl_trans} http://... $TEMP\...
