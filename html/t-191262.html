It's confusing at first, but works nicely in the end...


Define your strings in both languages...


LangString MY_WELCOME_TEXT ${LANG_ENGLISH} "English Text"
LangString MY_WELCOME_INFO_TITLE ${LANG_ENGLISH} "English Text"
LangString MY_WELCOME_INFO_TEXT ${LANG_ENGLISH} "English Text"
LangString MY_MSGBOX ${LANG_ENGLISH} "English Text"

LangString MY_WELCOME_TEXT ${LANG_GERMAN} "German Text"
LangString MY_WELCOME_INFO_TITLE ${LANG_GERMAN} "German Text"
LangString MY_WELCOME_INFO_TEXT ${LANG_GERMAN} "German Text"
LangString MY_MSGBOX ${LANG_GERMAN} "German Text"

Then use this to define the MUI, Messagebox, or Interface Texts

LangString WELCOME_TEXT "$(MY_WELCOME_TEXT)"
LangString MUI_TEXT_WELCOME_INFO_TITLE "$(MY_WELCOME_INFO_TITLE)"
LangString MUI_TEXT_WELCOME_INFO_TEXT "$(MY_WELCOME_INFO_TEXT)"

Messagebox mb_ok "$(My_MSGBOX)" /SD IDOK
