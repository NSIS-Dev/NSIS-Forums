these commands don't go inside an function
Why not? You con declare variables

var bbb
!define MUI_WELCOMEPAGE_TITLE "$ttt"
!define MUI_WELCOMEPAGE_TEXT "$bbb"

StrCpy $ttt "asdf"
StrCpy $bbb "zxcv"
