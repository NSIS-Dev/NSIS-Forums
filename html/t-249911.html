MUI uses MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_DISPLAY to mask InstallOptions::Dialog.
Just use InstallOptionsEx::Dialog instead.
You can still use the other MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_* macros though (like READ, EXTRACT etc)
