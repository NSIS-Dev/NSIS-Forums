ReadRegStr must be inside of Section or Function.
Your error was for the "Version"="2.0" thing...
Try this little exampleFor example:


Function .onInit
ReadRegStr $0 HKLM "SOFTWARE\Microsoft\DirectX" "Version"
StrCpy $1 $0 4 3
MessageBox MB_OK $1
Quit
FunctionEnd
