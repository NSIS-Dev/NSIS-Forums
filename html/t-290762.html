Unfortunately it appears that the HMNE project has been abandoned by the author.
Since it is open source, perhaps someone who is interested in maintaining (and updating) it can contact the author and perhaps take over the project.
HMNE is a very useful standalone editor for NSIS scripts.

Meanwhile, the next EclipseNSIS release will account for the new location of the MUI language nsh files. However, the EclipseNSIS Script Wizard continues to function correctly even when it cannot find the nsh files (it falls back upon the nlf files instead).
