Put !define MUI_PAGE_CUSTOMFUNCTION_PRE "PreFunc" in before the finish page command. Then insert this code:

Function "PreFunc"
!insertmacro MUI_INSTALLOPTIONS_WRITE "iospecial.ini" "Settings" "Cancelenabled" "0"
FunctionEnd
