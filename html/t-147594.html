I got it. I have to use:

LicenseData /LANG=${LANG_ENGLISH} "res\gpl.txt"
LicenseData /LANG=${LANG_GERMAN} "res\gpl.txt"
LicenseData /LANG=${LANG_FRENCH} "res\gpl.txt"
LicenseData /LANG=${LANG_SPANISH} "res\gpl.txt"
