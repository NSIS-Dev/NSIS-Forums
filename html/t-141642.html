try this:

!definestOSVERSIONINFO'(&l4,i,i,i,i,&t128)i'

!definesysGetVersionEx'kernel32::GetVersionExA(i)i'


System::Call'*${stOSVERSIONINFO}(,,,,,).r0'

System::Call'${sysGetVersionEx}(r0)'

;Getinto$1theservicepackinfo

System::Call'*$0${stOSVERSIONINFO}(,,,,,.r1)'



DetailPrint"$1"

System::Free$0
PS: I'm not an expert with system.dll plugin, but I want to be :D

good luck,
