In order to get Japanese to show up on an English copy of XP, you need to change the language for non-Unicode programs. The safe way to detect when you may display Japanese would be to detect the Japanese code page being active, otherwise don't display it.

EDIT:
You can change that by going to your Regional Settings->Advanced.
