I got it, - the parameters MUSt be in a separate string completely from the string.

So, this won't work:
CreateShortCut "$SMPROGRAMS\VMS Images\Open Images folder.lnk" "$WINDIR\explorer.exe /root,c:\VMS images"

But this will:
CreateShortCut "$SMPROGRAMS\VMS Images\Open Images folder.lnk" "$WINDIR\explorer.exe " "/root,c:\VMS images"
