kichik
19th May 2006 12:52 UTC

You can use a macro.

!macro myfunc un
Function ${un}myfunc
  Call ${un}someotherfunc
  DetailPrint something
FunctionEnd
!macroend

!insertmacro myfunc ""
!insertmacro myfunc "un."
