try this

PHP Code:

ClearErrors

Loop:
${ConfigRead}"old.ini""***91;AETITLE"$var;setserrorflagifnotfound
IfErrorsEndLoop
StrCpy$var$var-1;deletesthe"***93;"
 WriteIniStr"new.ini""network"$var"whatyouwanttoadd"
 DeleteINISec"old.ini""***91;AETITLE$var***93;"
 GotoLoop
EndLoop: the DeleteIniSec part is probably undesired and clumsy, but you get the idea with the loop
