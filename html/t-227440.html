Put !insertmacro MUI_LANGUAGE "English" after all your !insertmacro MUI_UNPAGE_...

Also, !include "${NSISDIR}\Contrib\Modern UI\System.nsh" is not really necessary. You can use !include "MUI.nsh" to do the same thing.
