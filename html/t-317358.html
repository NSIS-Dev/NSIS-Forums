It's a variable, so you can change it like any other variable. StrCpy, ReadRegStr, ReadINIStr, ...

Section MainFiles

StrCpy $INSTDIR "$PROGRAMFILES\Foo\Bar"

SetOutPath "$INSTDIR"
...

SectionEnd
