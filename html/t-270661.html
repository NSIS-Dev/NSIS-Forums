Following ini ripped from Firefox installation (hopefully they don't mind), might help you

[Settings]
RTL=0
NumFields=4
[Field 1]
Type=label
Text=Create icons for Firefox:
Left=0
Right=-1
Top=5
Bottom=15

[Field 2]
Type=checkbox
Text=On my &Desktop
Left=15
Right=-1
Top=20
Bottom=30
State=1
Flags=GROUP

[Field 3]
Type=checkbox
Text=In my &Start Menu Programs folder
Left=15
Right=-1
Top=40
Bottom=50
State=1

[Field 4]
Type=checkbox
Text=In my &Quick Launch bar
Left=15
Right=-1
Top=60
Bottom=70
State=1
