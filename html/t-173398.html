4.9.4.19 StrCmp
str1 str2 jump_if_equal [jump_if_not_equal]

Compares (case insensitively) str1 to str2. If str1 and str2 are equal, Gotos jump_if_equal, otherwise Gotos jump_if_not_equal.
I.e.:

StrCmp "OH" "blablabla" 0 +10
