ExecShell open "http://www.mywebsite.com/"

NSIS doesn't use anymore MUI_PRODUCT to define a product name, instead, it uses $(^Name) language string. So:

MessageBox MB_YESNO|MB_ICONQUESTION "Open $(^Name) website?" IDNO +2
