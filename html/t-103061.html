NSIS Workbench Source Available
I've made the source code for the NSIS Workbench (FNSIS) available.

FNSIS is coded in Delphi and uses both freeware and commercial components (I've replaced a couple of commercial ones with standard controls) See the "Readme For Developers.txt" for details.

It's available from: www.techmarc.co.uk/fnsis/fnsis-os.zip

At the moment there's no license included but I ask for copies of any enhancements to the source.

If you need any pointers just drop me a line.
