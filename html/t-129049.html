Haven't tested it but it should work:


GetFileName

  Exch$0 ; input string

  Push$1

  Push$2

  StrCpy$1 0

  loop:

   IntOp $1 $1 - 1

    StrCpy$2 $0 1 $1

    StrCmp$2 "" exit_dec

    StrCmp$2 "\" exit

    Goto loop

  exit_dec:

    IntOp $1 $1 - 1

  exit:

    IntOp $1 $1 + 1

    StrCpy $0 $0 "" $1

    Pop $2

    Pop $1

    Exch $0

FunctionEnd
