Bug? Maybe..

But, here's a workaround (or a solution, depending on how you look it!)


name "test backslash"
outfile "Test Backslash.exe"
var mypath
var dol 
Section
Sectionend

function .oninit
   Setsilent silent
   strcpy $dol "$$"
   strcpy $mypath "\\filesrv01\myfolder$dol\tnifile.ini"
   messagebox MB_OK "$$mypath = $mypath"
FunctionEnd
