FindProc and MUI
Is there an issue using FindProcDLL and the MUI?

FindProcDLL::FindProc "MyApp.exe"
StrCmp $R0 1 0 +3
MessageBox MB_OK "Close MyApp and retry this install."
Quit

You could try the old fashioned way:
