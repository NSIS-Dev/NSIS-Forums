For Apache 2.0.39 it's HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Uninstall\{3A862C7D-0504-48BC-AEF8-7F7479C7C158}

Uninstall:
MsiExec.exe /I{3A862C7D-0504-48BC-AEF8-7F7479C7C158}
