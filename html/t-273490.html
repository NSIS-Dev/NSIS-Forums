Also, what if the program launch fails? You will have an infinite loop on your hands.


StrCpy $1 0
loop:
  StrCmp $1 10000 exit
  IntOp $1 $1 + 1
  Sleep 500
  FindWindow $0 "" "xxx"
StrCmp $0 0 loop
exit:

System::Call 'user32::SetForegroundWindow(i r0)'
newadvsplash::stop
