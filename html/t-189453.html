For those who care, I worked out what to do:

GetDlgItem $0 $HWNDPARENT 1028
System::Call 'User32::SetWindowPos(i, i, i, i, i, i, i) b ($0, 0, ${X}, ${Y}, 0, 0, 0x0001)'
