Currently, there's only .onSelChange. But you can one-up Afrow's script with LogicLib.

!include LogicLib.nsh
!include Sections.nsh
#...
${If} ${SectionIsSelected} ${sec01}
  ${SelectSection} ${sec02}
${EndIf}
