rjstephens
9th February 2004 08:15 UTC

there's a parameter you can give to it to make the dialog box silent. In the macro code, change the line

---
CopyFiles ${FilePath} ${TargetDir}
---

to

---
CopyFiles /SILENT ${FilePath} ${TargetDir}
---

\and try it.
