NSIS on Twitter
automatically posts:

-new nsis releases (official, unicode and x64
-nsis questions on stackoverflow.com
-nsis bugs, feature requests, patches
-new releases for several nsis editors and other tools
-new files and talk from the nsis wiki

if you have more suggestions, feel free to share. anything with an atom/rss feed is easy to add.
