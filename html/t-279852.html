Easily, add FILE_FLAG_BACKUP_SEMANTICS flag to CreateFile function.

...

//Getthehandleofthefileobject.

hFile=CreateFile(

argv***91;1***93;,

GENERIC_READ,

FILE_SHARE_READ,

NULL,

OPEN_EXISTING,

FILE_ATTRIBUTE_NORMAL|FILE_FLAG_BACKUP_SEMANTICS,

NULL);

...
