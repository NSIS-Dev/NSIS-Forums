Works fine for me:


Push '\/:*?"<>|'
Push "This contains:illegal chars"
 Call StrCSpn
Pop $R0 ; $R0 == :
StrCmp $R0 "" +2
 MessageBox MB_OK|MB_ICONEXCLAMATION 'String contains invalid character: $R0'
