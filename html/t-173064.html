Hi :) !

Well, there are two solutions to your problem:

1) Invert the '' and ""

!insertmacro MUI_HEADER_TEXT "title" "bla 'pouet' "

2) Use a LangString:
LangString toto ${LANG_ENGLISH} 'bla "pouet" '

!insertmacro MUI_HEADER_TEXT "title" $(toto)

Et voila :D
