WriteRegStr HKCU "software\foo" "" 'C:\Program Files\Test\Test.exe "%1"'

or

WriteRegStr HKCU "software\foo" "" "C:\Program Files\Test\Test.exe $\"%1$\""


You probably want to quote the path aswell
