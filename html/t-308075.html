It's pretty simple:

ReadEnvStr $R0 COMSPEC
nsExec::ExecToLog `"$R0" /C "$INSTDIR\exec.bat"`
Pop $R0

You can also use ExecDos::exec /DETAILED which I prefer:
http://nsis.sourceforge.net/ExecDos_plug-in
