Instructor
8th May 2005 16:01 UTC

Section Uninstall
        label1:
        GetLabelAddress $0 label1
        IntOp $0 $0 + 3
        goto a
        ;...

        label2:
        GetLabelAddress $0 label2
        IntOp $0 $0 + 3
        goto a
        ;...
        goto end

        a:
        ;...
        goto $0

        end:
SectionEnd
