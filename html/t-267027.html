$LANGUAGE is a variable and not a define and it's runtime and not compile time. Use $LANGUAGE instead of ${LANGUAGE} and ${If} or StrCmp instead of !if.

!include LogicLib.nsh
#...
${If} $LANGUAGE == ${LANG_GERMAN}
#...
${EndIf}
