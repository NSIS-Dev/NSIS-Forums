With your suggesstion a, I was able to hide close and cancel button by scripting as below

For Close
GetDlgItem $R0 $HWNDPARENT 1
ShowWindow $R0 ${SW_HIDE}

For cancel
GetDlgItem $R0 $HWNDPARENT 2
ShowWindow $R0 ${SW_HIDE}
