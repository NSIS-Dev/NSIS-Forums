This should work:

ShowWindow $hCtl_RenameMe_Locations 0

Where $hCtl_RenameMe_Locations is the DropDownList variable you Popped after creating it, and 0 is SW_HIDE. http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/libr...(v=vs.85).aspx
