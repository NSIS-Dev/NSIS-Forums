Afrow UK
21st December 2006 12:51 UTC

StrCmp $R0 "" +2
IfFileExists "$R0\*.*" +3
 MessageBox MB_OK|MB_ICONSTOP "Bad path!"
 Abort

Assuming $R0 is the path read from the INI file.
