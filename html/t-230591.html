The best thing for you to do would probably be to contact the company that you bought the game from. NSIS performs a CRC check (Cyclic Redundancy Check) before every startup. This ensures that the file was downloaded correctly and that it is not infected with a virus or anything. If you have had trouble downloading files from different websites, you may want to check your Internet connection also.

If you let the company know the full text of the error message and point them to this thread, they should be willing to send you a new download link.
