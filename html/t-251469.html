It's MUI_PAGE_CUSTOMFUNCTION_SHOW, not MUI_CUSTOMFUNCTION_DIRECTORY_SHOW. Plus, you shouldn't define MUI_CUSTOMPAGECOMMANDS or MUI_DIRECTORYPAGE. Follow the readme, don't get defines from System.nsh.
