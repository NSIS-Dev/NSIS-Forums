Originally posted by ssurfer26
I'm talking about any icon. Not just the nsis onen. The icon in the window, the icons in the selection of options etc.
By changing the one main icon to high colour, the one in the window will change as well. You just need to find 766 in the source and replace it with however big a high or true colour icon is.

The section selection :igor: box uses bitmaps, so that should be a case of going through the source, finding 358 and replacing it with 1254.
