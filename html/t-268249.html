Everything should just work. To get rid of the "unknown publisher" you do need to pay someone to get a certificate. To get rid of the uninstaller warning, you should use RequestExecutionLevel which will add a manifest that'd cause Vista to avoid its automatic backward compatibility tricks.

There's also the issue of applications executed from the installer executing in the same context, the administrator context and not the user context. Andres has some solutions for that.
