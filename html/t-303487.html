ok here is my solution :
to install codec :
ExecWait 'rundll32.exe advpack.dll,LaunchINFSectionEx .\xvid.inf,,,4'

to uninstall :
ExecWait 'rundll32.exe setupapi,InstallHinfSection Remove_XviD 132 C:\WINDOWS\INF\xvid.inf'
